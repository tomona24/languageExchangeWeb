About

Japanese x English Language Exchange

We help you study both Japanese and English.

Are you seeking a community to learn the languages?
Are you a native Japanese or English speaker?
This is the group for you.

Focusing on studying

We build our language skills as talking with others. We study a lot of vocabrary, natural expressions, and cultual differences through our conversation.

Friendly people

You can get a lot of friends who seriously study Japanese and English skills. Everyone is such a hard learner and a supporter to improve your language skills as well.

Helpful activity

Our meetup group does not offer the opportunities just to talk with others. We have an activity in each session to help you study efficiently.

Activity

Videos on Youtube

We are facing a lot of things through our lives, but we do not know the names and the expressions. We watch a short video on Youtube and learn how to express everything that is shown on it.

Learn natural words and expression

Through this activity, you can learn not only basic vocabrary but also advanced expressions. It helps you understand the difference and features between each culture and each language.

Contact

Even though we know some words in books, movies, and songs, we do not know all words we can see in our daily lives. We do not have enough opportunities to know them by ourselves. That's why, I made this meetup group for people who are eager to increase their vocabrary.
